Waveform data attribution and license
=====================================

The waveform CSV files in `waveform_data/herbert/` and
`waveform_data/p003914/` are extracts from the MIMIC-III Waveform Database,
distributed under the Open Data Commons Open Database License (ODbL) v1.0.

Required citations (please include in any redistribution or publication):

  Moody, B., Moody, G., Villarroel, M., Clifford, G. D., & Silva, I. (2020).
  MIMIC-III Waveform Database (version 1.0). PhysioNet.
  https://doi.org/10.13026/c2607m

  Johnson, A. E. W., Pollard, T. J., Shen, L., Lehman, L. H., Feng, M.,
  Ghassemi, M., Moody, B., Szolovits, P., Celi, L. A., & Mark, R. G. (2016).
  MIMIC-III, a freely accessible critical care database.
  Scientific Data, 3, 160035.

  Goldberger, A., Amaral, L., Glass, L., Hausdorff, J., Ivanov, P. C.,
  Mark, R., ... & Stanley, H. E. (2000). PhysioBank, PhysioToolkit, and
  PhysioNet: Components of a new research resource for complex physiologic
  signals. Circulation, 101(23), e215-e220.

ODbL v1.0 full text:
  https://opendatacommons.org/licenses/odbl/1-0/

Key obligations summary (not legal advice):
  - Attribution: credit the source databases as cited above.
  - Share-Alike: any derivative database must be released under ODbL.
  - Keep open: redistributions must preserve this notice.

These obligations apply to the waveform data only. The simulator source
code in this repository is licensed separately under the MIT License.

The waveform clips here are short extracts (~20 seconds each) selected
to illustrate specific cardiac chambers during PAC insertion teaching.
The full source records remain available at PhysioNet under the same
license:
  https://physionet.org/content/mimic3wdb-matched/1.0/

Clinical narratives (vignette.txt files) shipped publicly with this
project are fictional teaching cases written for educational use. They
are NOT derived from individual patient records in the MIMIC-III
Clinical Database (which is access-controlled and cannot be
redistributed). Credentialed researchers may add their own clinical
content under waveform_data/<patient>/clinical_data/, which is
git-ignored.
